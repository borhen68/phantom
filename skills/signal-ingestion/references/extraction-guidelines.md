# Signal Extraction Guidelines

## Strong extraction signals

- explicit promises: "I'll send", "we will deliver", "need by Friday"
- clear roles: manager, customer, approver, owner
- named projects or recurring workstreams

## Weak extraction signals

- vague wishes with no commitment
- names with no clear relationship
- dates with no owner or action

When in doubt, keep the raw signal and avoid overcommitting the structured memory.
